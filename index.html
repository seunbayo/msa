What I do

MoneySense Coach

Money sense Academy is a community of income earning adults and busy professionals who are interested in managing their money and their,lives better by improving their income potential, saving more and multiplying their money so as to live a more peaceful and fulfilling life.

Our mission remains to help Africans understand, evaluate and relate with money in unconventional but empowering ways that lead to true wealth and fulfillment.
Our vision : Remains to have an Africa where #MoneySense is a thinking and decision making tool that people can leverage on to better their lives one step at a time.
Our goal : Remains to help Africans experience personal and financial possibilities by increasing their income potential, saving and managing their income better and effortlessly multiplying it through suitable and knowledge-driven opportunities.

Who I am

MoneySense Coach

Alex Iheanacho is an Amazon bestselling Author, trainer, Financial Intelligence Coach, Sensational speaker, businessman, investor and creator of The Money Sense Blueprint. He is the Chief Responsibility Officer of Money Sense Africa, a financial education and coaching company that helps individuals and organizations to improve their financial results through Financial Intelligence.

Alex is also an Associate of Lexnuel Solutions, a training company that helps create intervention programs for companies in the areas of marketing, peak performance and personal mastery, goal setting financial management, business communication with special interest in Storytelling and copywriting. Alex is in charge of Business Development for Barinaada legal, an SME focused law firm based in Lagos.

He founded STREET TO STARDOM FOUNDATION to help reach out to and transform the lives of hawkers. He has volunteered for African Union Agenda 2063 Project Lagos wing, Live your dream Africa conference and other projects. He was nominated as one of 100 most influential young Nigerians for 2018 by Avance media and an African Changemaker fellow cohort3.
